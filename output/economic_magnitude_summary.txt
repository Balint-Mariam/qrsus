Economic Magnitude / Standardized Shock Effects Summary
======================================================

Formula for non-standardized QR and rolling QR coefficients:
impact_1sd_bps = beta * sd(driver) * 10000

QLP note:
Existing QLP models use standardize_shocks=True, so main-driver QLP coefficients
are already responses to +1 sample-standard-deviation shocks. Therefore QLP
economic magnitudes are converted to bps as coef * 10000, not multiplied by SD again.

Interpretation notes:
- QR impacts are contemporaneous changes in the daily ESG-conventional spread.
- QLP impacts are cumulative future ESG-conventional spread changes over horizon h.
- Effects are economically scaled conditional associations, not causal impulse responses.
